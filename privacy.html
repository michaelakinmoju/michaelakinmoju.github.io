Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information on michaelakinmoju.com.

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Michael Akinmoju ("I", "me", or "my") operating under the brand UnlockWithMichael, collects, uses and protects information gathered through michaelakinmoju.com (the "Site").

By using this Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. I take your privacy seriously and am committed to protecting your personal information.

Information I Collect

I collect the following types of information when you visit or interact with the Site:

  • Contact information — your name, email address, phone number and project details when you submit the contact form
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent on pages, browser type and device information collected automatically via Google Analytics and Google AdSense
  • Cookies — small files stored on your device by Google AdSense and Analytics to personalise ads and measure site performance
  • IP address — collected automatically when you visit the Site for security and analytics purposes

How I Use Your Information

Information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries and project requests submitted via the contact form
  • To improve the Site's content, design and user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To analyse Site traffic and usage patterns via Google Analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations where required

I do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to third parties.

Google AdSense & Analytics

This Site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements and Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site. These services may use cookies and collect data about your browsing behaviour.

Google may use the information collected to personalise ads shown to you on this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google handles your data, please visit the Google Privacy Policy.

Cookies

This Site uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your experience and serve relevant advertisements. Cookies used on this Site include:

  • Google AdSense cookies — used to serve personalised ads based on your interests
  • Google Analytics cookies — used to track Site usage and visitor behaviour anonymously

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of the Site.

Third Party Services

This Site uses the following third party services that may collect information about you:

  • EmailJS — used to send contact form submissions to my email inbox. Your form data is transmitted securely through EmailJS servers.
  • Google AdSense — advertising platform that displays ads on the Site
  • Google Analytics — website analytics service
  • Google Fonts — font delivery service that may log requests
  • Font Awesome — icon library delivered via CDN

Data Security

I take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, alteration or disclosure. Contact form submissions are transmitted securely via EmailJS and are only accessible to me.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While I strive to protect your information, I cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this Site, please contact me and I will delete it promptly.

Your Rights (Canada — PIPEDA)

As a Canadian resident, under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information I hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent to the use of your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me at michealakinmoju@gmail.com.

External Links

This Site may contain links to external websites. I am not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. I encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. I encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continued use of the Site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
