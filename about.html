My Story

From curiosity to craft — building the web one site at a time.

My journey into web design started with a simple question: why do so many small businesses have websites that look outdated or unprofessional? I saw talented entrepreneurs, hardworking local business owners, and passionate founders struggling to make an impression online — not because of their product, but because their website didn't reflect the quality of their work.

That gap is what I set out to close. I started building websites for businesses in my community in Windsor, Ontario, and quickly realized that what I was doing was more than just a technical service — it was helping people unlock their potential online. That's where the name UnlockWithMichael was born.

Today I work with small businesses and startups across Canada, delivering custom-built websites that are fast, mobile-friendly, SEO-ready, and designed to turn visitors into paying clients — all starting at just $299. Every site I build is created from scratch with clean code. No templates. No shortcuts. Just real, thoughtful design that represents your brand the right way.

Beyond web design, I'm also a published author. My book Unchained explores personal growth and the mindset shifts that help people break free from limitations — themes that run through everything I do, including how I approach design and client relationships.
